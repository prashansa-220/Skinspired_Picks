Common causes of brittle, weak, or peeling nails include water exposure, nutrient deficiencies, harsh chemicals, and excessive use of nail products.

Recommendations:

  • Moisturize nails and cuticles daily with natural oils (e.g., jojoba, almond, vitamin E).
  • Wear gloves while doing household chores.
  • Avoid acetone-based removers and limit use of gel/acrylic nails.
